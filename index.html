Before You Start — Information Required

Please have the following information ready before opening the application form.

From the Agency

  • Agency Contact First Name
  • Agency Contact Last Name
  • Agency Contact Email Address
  • Agency Contact Phone Number

From the Intended Parent(s)

  • Primary IP First Name
  • Primary IP Last Name
  • Primary IP Date of Birth
  • Primary IP Phone Number
  • Primary IP Email Address
  • Primary IP Full Address
  • Secondary IP Name (optional)
  • Secondary IP Email (optional)
  • Deductible Selection
  • Policy Limit Selection
  • Escrow Account (if applicable: company name, phone & email)
  • Preferred Payment Method
  • Date of Embryo Transfer
  • Expected Due Date
  • Newborn Gender (if known)
  • Newborn Last Name

From the Surrogate & Delivery

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Full Address
  • Social Security Number
  • Health Screening:
  • BMI % (33.0 or below required)
  • At least one prior healthy pregnancy
  • Age 41+ at date of transfer
  • Prior history of:
    • Preterm labor before week 37
    • Pre-eclampsia or eclampsia
    • Diabetes
    • Gestational diabetes requiring hospitalization
    • Three or more prior c-sections
    • Six or more prior pregnancies
    • Delivery within the last 9 months
    • Miscarriage beyond 16 weeks
    • Placental abruption
    • Placental previa
  • Delivery Information:
  • OB Name & Phone Number
  • Delivery Facility Name, Phone & Full Address
  • Birth Plan
